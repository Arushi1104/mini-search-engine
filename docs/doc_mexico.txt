Mexico is a country in North America bordering the United States to the north. Mexico City is the capital and one of the largest cities in the world. Mexico has a rich ancient history including the Aztec and Maya civilizations. The country is famous for its cuisine including tacos, guacamole, and enchiladas. Mexico has a diverse economy driven by manufacturing, oil, and tourism. Chichen Itza is one of the most famous ancient ruins in the world.
