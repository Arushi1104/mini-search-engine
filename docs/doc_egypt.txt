Egypt is a country in North Africa and the Middle East. Cairo is the capital. Egypt is home to one of the oldest civilizations in the world. The ancient Egyptians built the Great Pyramids of Giza and the Sphinx which remain among the greatest architectural achievements in history. The Nile River is the longest river in the world and was essential to ancient Egyptian civilization. Egypt is also known for hieroglyphics, mummies, and pharaohs.
