South Africa is a country located at the southern tip of the African continent. Pretoria is the administrative capital. South Africa is known for its diverse landscapes including savannas, mountains, and coastlines. The country is famous for safari wildlife including lions, elephants, and rhinoceroses. South Africa has a complex history including apartheid and the leadership of Nelson Mandela who became a global symbol of peace and justice. The economy is driven by mining, especially gold and diamonds.
